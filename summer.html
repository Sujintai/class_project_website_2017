What Happens?
During the Summer, plants are green as ever and it only gets hotter. The summer brings the longest days and the shortest nights out of the entire year.
Wilderness with a view of the scorching Sun.
When is Summer?
The Summer time is from June to September in the Northern Hemisphere and December to March in the Southern Hemisphere.

How's the Weather?
The Summer is the hottest season of the year. During the Summer, in NYC it averages at around 75-95 degrees fahrenheit. It's crazy hot during the Summer. If you're trying to survive the Summer, it would be helpful to wear clothing that is light and short such as t-shirts or shorts.
