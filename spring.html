What Happens?
During the Spring, flowers start blooming again and plants start growing their leaves again because Spring comes right after Winter.
Some flowers.
When is Spring?
The Spring time is from March to June in the Northern hemisphere and September to December in the Southern Hemisphere. When it is Spring in the North, it is Fall in the South. When it is Fall in the North, it is Spring in the South.
Trees growing leaves during the Spring.
How's the Weather?
In New York City, Spring brings medium weather which is not too hot, nor too cold. It stays around 40-70 degrees fahrenheit on average. This is the kind of weather where you might want to wear something light like a sweater. Spring also brings rain, so have an umbrella handy.
